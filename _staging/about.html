About Our Foundation

Empowering underserved youth through Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, mentorship, and life skills development in North Dallas and Plano

Our Mission

The Cobra Youth Scholarship Foundation provides comprehensive Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu scholarships to underserved youth aged 4-26 in the North Dallas and Plano communities. We believe that every young person deserves access to high-quality martial arts training, mentorship, and life skills development regardless of their family's financial situation. Through our program, we're building stronger, more confident individuals who become positive leaders in their communities.

Our Story

The Cobra Youth Scholarship Foundation was born from a simple observation: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu has the power to transform young lives, but the cost of training often puts it out of reach for the families who could benefit most.

Founded in 2023 by a group of BJJ practitioners, parents, and community leaders in North Dallas, we saw firsthand how martial arts training builds confidence, discipline, and resilience in young people. We also saw too many talented youth unable to participate due to financial barriers.

What started as a small effort to sponsor a few local kids has grown into a comprehensive scholarship program that provides not just tuition assistance, but complete support including gear, mentorship, and life skills development. Today, we're proud to partner with multiple academies throughout North Dallas and Plano, serving over 140 youth and their families.
