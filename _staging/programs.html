Our Programs

Comprehensive Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training, mentorship, and life skills development for underserved youth in North Dallas and Plano

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Training

Professional instruction in the gentle art of BJJ, focusing on technique, discipline, and personal growth.

  • Classes 2-3 times per week
  • Age-appropriate curriculum
  • Focus on self-defense and fitness
  • Competition opportunities
  • Belt progression system
  • Character development through martial arts

Mentorship Program

One-on-one guidance from experienced mentors who provide support beyond the mat.

  • Paired with dedicated mentors
  • Academic support and tutoring
  • Goal setting and achievement
  • Life skills development
  • Career guidance and planning
  • Personal development coaching

Life Skills Development

Essential skills training that builds confidence, leadership, and prepares youth for success.

  • Leadership training workshops
  • Communication skills development
  • Financial literacy education
  • Conflict resolution techniques
  • Time management and organization
  • College and career preparation

Full Scholarship Support

Complete coverage of training costs, equipment, and competition fees for qualifying youth.

  • Free monthly tuition
  • Uniform and gear provided
  • Competition entry fees covered
  • Transportation assistance
  • Nutritional support when needed
  • Long-term commitment support
