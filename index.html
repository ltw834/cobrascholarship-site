Changing Lives Through Jiu-Jitsu

The Cobra Youth Scholarship Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting kids in our community. Through Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, we give underserved youth the chance to grow stronger, more confident, and more disciplined—while gaining life skills and mentorship that last a lifetime.

What We Do

We transform lives by opening doors that poverty often closes. Many of the students we serve cannot afford after-school programs or sports, leaving them without safe and structured opportunities to grow. With your support, the Cobra Youth Scholarship Foundation removes financial barriers and gives them a path to confidence, discipline, and resilience through Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Where We Serve

We serve students throughout the North Dallas area, with training taking place in Plano, TX in partnership with Cobra BJJ, a professional martial arts academy.

Cobra BJJ provides world-class instruction in a safe, supportive environment where students learn respect, discipline, and perseverance alongside technical martial arts skills.

Who Qualifies

Scholarships are awarded to youth and young adults ages 4-26 living in the North Dallas area, with priority given to:

  • Low-income households facing financial barriers to extracurricular activities
  • Single-parent families seeking positive mentorship and structure for their children
  • Youth facing challenging circumstances who would benefit from a supportive community
  • Students committed to growth in both martial arts and personal development

We believe every child deserves access to quality mentorship and the life-changing benefits of martial arts training, regardless of their family's financial situation.
